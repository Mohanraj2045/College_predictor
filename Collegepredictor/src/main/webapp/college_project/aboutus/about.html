Predi-Tech

We are a team of students from Rajalakshmi Engineering College who have implemented a machine learning model to help students understand their chances of getting into a particular college. Our model uses key factors such as 12th-grade marks in Maths, Chemistry, and Physics, along with other features like community, to predict probabilities of college admission. Our goal is to empower students with data-driven insights to make informed decisions about their education.
